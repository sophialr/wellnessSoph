About Me

This is what I do.

My name is Sophia Reed, and I am a rising sophomore at the University of Michigan. I became passionate in nutrition and well being along side my passion for computers. Combing the two, I strive to build a website through my coding background that can help people learn and grow.

With all the different fitness/food trends, tips, and strategies to gain or lose weight, it can be overwhelming. As you go through the feed, find what interests you. It is time to have fun and experiment with tips that seem beneficial and introduce new foods in your life. Everyone is different and there is no one size fits all diet. Take some tips I provide here whatever it may be and make your own recipe of healthy and happy living.

Remember to keep moving, focus on the good, and be kind.
